AI Assistant

for Overleaf

Stored locally in your browser. Sent only to the selected provider's API.

How to use

  1. Open a project on overleaf.com
  2. The AI sidebar appears on the right side of the editor
  3. Choose Proofreading or Style mode
  4. Click Check Document or select text and click Check Selection
  5. Suggestions appear in the sidebar and as inline highlights
